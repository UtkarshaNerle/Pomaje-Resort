🌾 About Pomaje Resort

Where Village Charm
Meets Natural Serenity

Pomaje Resort – Chikuchi Wadi is a peaceful pure-veg family destination set in the heart of Majarewadi village, just minutes from Kurundwad, Kolhapur. Surrounded by verdant hills, flowing streams, and lush paddy fields, the resort invites you to unwind, reconnect with nature, and relish authentic Maharashtrian hospitality.

Whether you're here for a family getaway, a birthday celebration, or simply to breathe fresh country air — our resort offers the perfect blend of traditional village vibes and comfortable modern amenities.
